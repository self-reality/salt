## Launch Sequence

### 1. Discover
Cans are revealed gradually. Artists and owners receive a Preview NFT — and must find it in their spam.

### 2. Expertise
All cans cost the same. The price per can is determined collectively. Owners get access to a closed sale.

### 3. Ship
Collection launch.

---

## Can Lifecycle

### Discover
White label cans go on sale. Each white label can is assigned an artist, but the buyer doesn't know which one until purchase.

### Expertise. Reveal & Seal
Each artist has one work. Each work has 12 cans. Buying at least one reveals what's inside — and the rest of that batch.

Sealed = contains the artwork. White label = the can before reveal.

### Ship
You can choose how many to buy upfront. But you don't know what's inside. Purchase reveals.
